LiftIt

LiftIt was a project devised to solve the problem many people have when going to the gym. What do I work out? How do I do the exercise? I dont have the motivation, blah blah blah. You've heard it all. However we created this app to save all of that. Struggling to figure out what to work out is a thing of the past. The app is operated actually quite simply for the user so there is no confusion. My team and I wanted to have a streamline look. You start by picking exactly what body part you want to work out, after clicking that body part you're met with many options exact body movements. You can then repeat this process until you have your desired workout picked.

RateMyEmployees

RateMyEmployees was a fun app we came up with after discussing work ethic and co-workers in the workplace. This awesome app has a few different unique features. However the barebones of it is this, we wanted companies to be able to keep track of their employees. Within companies there are constantly people coming and going, with our app it helps to keep track of those trying to promote through the ranks. It helps managers to see what the employee is good at, with a simple star rating system it is easy to know if you want them on board or not. We have a simple add feature with a delete feature to come for the employees that leave the company. We would also like to add special logins for each company that uses our app.
